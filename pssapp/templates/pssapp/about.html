Prudent Services and Solutions

Prudent is in the business of securing people & premises with man guarding services and believes to provide quality services to their clients and employees and certified ISO 9001:2015 on Quality Management System.

Our business is partnering with various commercials, Industrials, residential and corporates in urban and remote sites with a diversified area of Gujarat, Daman & Diu and Dadra and Nagar Haveli.

We are aspiring to expand our operations into Unskilled & Skilled manpower management, facility management, electronic surveillance services in near future.
