Health & Safety

Along with business growth, Prudent strongly believe to maintain health & safety for their employees at all levels. Feeling safe at work is a top priority for overall job satisfaction and employee retention. To maintain a safe workplace Prudent also provides safety and security training.

1.) TOOL BOX TALK

Safety toolbox talks are short 5 - 15 minutes safety talks & Videos that refresh knowledge. And they’re an excellent way to keep safety front of mind for employees. It is done during visits by Prudent Officials regularly at sites.

2.) SAFETY WALK AND TALK(SWAT)

Safety walks are just a observational walks where we find hazard and risk for employee at work place to prevent minor/major accidents. Done by Prudent officials on monthly basis.

3.) HAZARD REPORTING

We keep identifying hazards from sites to avoid the incidents and major/minor accidents at workplace. Priority closure of hazard with the help of management and employees makes the work place safer.
